LT™ Locating System

  • Products
  • LT™ Locating System

Designed for maximum versatility in HDD operations, the DigiTrak® LT™ Locating System is lightweight, very fast, and simple to use.

While it is well suited for shallow depths and areas where compact equipment is required, the DigiTrak LT system has an impressive 33-ft (10.1-m) range and features locating technologies that reflect DCI’s most current innovations.

  • Versatile applications
  • Compact, lightweight design
  • Advanced circuitry for enhanced roll/pitch speed
  • Menu-driven operation
  • Left/right and up/down remote steering
